## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Prepare the following:
- A valid email address for account creation
- An Excel file (.xlsx or .csv) with your initial contacts (columns: `name`, `email`, `phone`)
- Access to your business email for verification
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up at `https://dashboard.amplifiqueme.com` and verify your email to access the dashboard. This takes under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to the dashboard URL and click **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your details:

    | Field       | Example Value              |
    |-------------|----------------------------|
    | Email       | yourname@business.com      |
    | Password    | StrongPassword123!         |
    | Company     | Your Business Name         |

    Submit the form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from Amplifique.me.

    Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Import Contacts via Excel

Upload your contact list to start collecting feedback.

<Callout kind="tip">
Export your contacts from your CRM as Excel or CSV first.
</Callout>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Excel (.xlsx)" icon="file-spreadsheet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Prepare File">
        Ensure your Excel has headers: `name`, `email`, `phone`, `tags`.

        Example structure:
````csv
name,email,phone,tags
John Doe,john@company.com,+5511999999999,VIP
Jane Smith,jane@company.com,+5511888888888,Standard
````
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload">
        Go to **Contacts** > **Import**.

        Drag and drop your file or click **Browse**.

        Map columns if prompted, then **Import**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CSV" icon="file-text">
    Use the same structure as shown above. Save as UTF-8 to avoid encoding issues.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Build and Send Your First Survey

Create a simple survey targeting your imported contacts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Survey" icon="plus">
    Navigate to **Surveys** > **New Survey**.

    Add questions like:

    - "How satisfied are you? (1-5 scale)"
    - "What can we improve? (open text)"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Target Contacts" icon="users">
    Select your imported list or apply filters (e.g., by `tags`).

    Set delivery: Email or In-App.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send" icon="send">
    Review and click **Send Survey**.

    Track opens and responses in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the Dashboard

After sending, view metrics at `https://dashboard.amplifiqueme.com/dashboard`.

Key sections:
- **Metrics**: Response rates, satisfaction scores
- **Insights**: AI-powered analysis of feedback
- **Contacts**: Manage lists and tags

<Expandable title="Basic Navigation Tips" default-open="true">
  - Use the search bar for quick access
  - Apply filters: `>80%` satisfaction, date ranges
  - Export reports as PDF/CSV
</Expandable>
