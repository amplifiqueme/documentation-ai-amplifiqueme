## Overview

Amplifique.me helps you collect customer feedback through customizable surveys, analyze responses with AI-powered insights, and manage contacts efficiently. You gain actionable metrics to enhance business interactions and customer satisfaction. Access your dashboard at [https://dashboard.amplifique.me](https://dashboard.amplifique.me).

## Quick Start

Follow these steps to launch your first survey.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at [https://dashboard.amplifique.me](https://dashboard.amplifique.me) and verify your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Survey" icon="edit-3">
    Navigate to the Surveys section and build your first questionnaire using the drag-and-drop builder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Contacts" icon="upload">
    Upload your contact list via Excel or add them manually.

````bash
# Example CSV format for import
name,email,phone
John Doe,john@example.com,+5511999999999
Jane Smith,jane@example.com,+5511888888888
````

  </Step>
  <Step title="Send and Analyze" icon="send">
    Dispatch the survey and review AI-powered insights in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
