## Overview

To start with Amplifique.me, simply create a new project from your dashboard.

## Steps

1. Log in at `https://app.amplifique.me`
2. Click **New Project**
3. Fill in project name and details
4. Click **Create**

Your project will appear on your home screen. You can now add documentation sections and invite team members.
