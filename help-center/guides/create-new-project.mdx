## Start a Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Log in at `https://app.amplifique.me`, and go to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Click **New Project** in the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Details">
    Set a project name, and (optionally) a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click **Create**, and your project will be listed on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin Documenting">
    Click into the project to add sections and documentation pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
